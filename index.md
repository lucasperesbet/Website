### Poem

The poem is a sealed environment for words where their meaning develop away from words not chosen by the poet.

```
The dreamer had it right
The dreamer had it
The dreamer
The dream, and
Instead -
We'll dwell in faliure
And rejoice
In the absence of meaning
Awake.
```
